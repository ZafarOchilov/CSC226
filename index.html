BMW M5 Competition F90

The BMW M5 Competition F90 is an extraordinary blend of luxury and performance. It showcases BMW's commitment to engineering excellence and provides an exhilarating driving experience.

BMW M5 Competition F90 specs

  • The M5 Competition F90 produces 617 horsepower from its 4.4-liter twin-turbo V8 engine, weighing at about 4,096 lbs.
  • It can accelerate from 0 to 60 mph in just 3.1 seconds.
  • Despite its high performance, it offers luxurious features like a leather interior and advanced driver assistance systems.
